I · Foundational statement

The discipline before the institution.

Norynthe was not founded to build another artificial intelligence system.

It was founded to understand trustworthy inference.

The Papers preserve the philosophical, scientific, and institutional foundations of that work.

No volume is considered final. Each edition remains open to revision as evidence, methodology, and understanding evolve.

II · Publications

Foundational publications

Volume I

On Trust, Inference, and Intelligence

A Founding Treatise

Volume I establishes the first principles of Norynthe: that intelligence and trust are different, that inference should become an object of science, and that the future of AI evaluation depends upon disciplined observation, living benchmarks, evolving rubrics, independent infrastructure, and institutional memory.
